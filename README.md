# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Segmentation Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
Analyzed 100,000+ real orders from Olist, Brazil's largest 
e-commerce platform, to uncover revenue trends, delivery 
bottlenecks, and customer loyalty patterns.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- MySQL — data cleaning & analysis across 8 tables
- Power BI — 5-page interactive dashboard

## 📊 Key Insights
- 💰 Health & Beauty is the #1 revenue category at $1.26M
- 🚚 SP state has fastest delivery at 8.7 days avg
- ⚠️ 8.11% of orders arrive late — 1 in 12 customers affected
- 👥 Only 3% of customers are Champions
- 💡 Converting At Risk customers could recover $500K+

## 📁 Dashboard Pages
1. Sales Overview — Monthly revenue & order trends
2. Top Categories — Revenue by product category
3. Delivery Analysis — Delivery speed by state
4. Late Deliveries — On-time vs late breakdown
5. Customer Segments — RFM segmentation analysis
